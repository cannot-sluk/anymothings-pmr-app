## Rotation Recommendations

### Ideal Weekly Rotation:
- **Day 1-2:** Chicken (with appropriate organs/bones)
- **Day 3-4:** Beef or Duck
- **Day 5-6:** Rabbit or Venison
- **Day 7:** Fish (optional, not more than once/week)

### Why Rotate?
- Different amino acid profiles
- Varied mineral content
- Prevents food sensitivities
- Ensures nutritional diversity
- Mimics natural prey variety

### Minimum Rotation:
- At least 2-3 different proteins per week
- Don't feed single protein for more than 2 weeks
- Introduce new proteins gradually (mix with familiar ones)

## HK-Specific Sourcing Tips

### Where to Buy in Hong Kong:

**Chicken & Duck:**
- Supermarkets (convenient, quality-controlled)
- Pet stores (pre-portioned, hormone-free options)
- Royal Farm (hormone-free certified)

- Supermarkets (frozen options, traceable)
- Butchers (can request specific cuts)
- Midfield products (quality assured)

**Rabbit:**
- Online suppliers (French imports recommended)
- Specialty pet stores
- Certified importers

**Venison:**
- Specialty pet stores
- Online raw food suppliers
- Imported frozen (more expensive)
