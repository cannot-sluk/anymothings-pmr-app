## Pricing Psychology for HK Market

### Sweet Spots:
- **Micro:** HK$38-88 (impulse buy)
- **Small:** HK$128-288 (considered purchase)
- **Medium:** HK$500-1000 (investment decision)
- **Premium:** HK$1500-3000 (serious commitment)

### Tips:
- Use HK$ pricing (not USD)
- Offer PayMe/FPS (popular in HK)
- Consider Octopus/Alipay for physical products
- Bundle pricing works well
- Early bird discounts create urgency

---

## Legal Considerations (Hong Kong)

### Must Have:
- Business Registration (if revenue > HK$1M/year)
- Proper tax reporting
- Clear terms of service
- Refund policy
- Privacy policy (even for free service)
- Professional indemnity insurance (for consultations)

### Disclaimers:
- Not a substitute for veterinary care
- Educational purposes only
- Individual results may vary
- Consult professionals before changes
